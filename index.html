Who I Am

I'm an IT Specialist student developer at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Maragondon Campus, focused on building real, production-grade systems for my campus community — not just classroom exercises.

My core work centers on vanilla PHP and MySQL: a full-scale e-voting and event tabulation platform used for actual student elections, and a Learning Management System that helps professors manage grades, attendance, and activities. Every project is built without frameworks, prioritizing reliability and maintainability for real institutional use.

Outside of capstone work, I build browser games, interactive campus maps, and explore web development as both a craft and a way to ship things people actually use. I believe in writing clean, dependable code over flashy shortcuts.
